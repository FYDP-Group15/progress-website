fydp group 15

week 3 | patents and solutions

september 30 - october 6

This week's goal was to identify a few possible solutions alongside current patented products in the market.

Solutions: • A pair of glasses using transparent OLED screens to displaying blindspot warnings for users. • Attaching a transparent OLED display to the front of the helmet incorporating blindspot warnings as well as additional collision prevention related info due to the larger screen size • Creating a dashboard to attach to the handle bars of a bike that would serve a similar purpose to a motorcycle bike dashboard. Providing all the collision prevention techniques of the glasses and helmet, alongside real time mapping of cyclist's location on the road.
