fydp group 15

week 1 | identifying need

september 16 - 22

After finalizing team members, the remainder of the week was spent discussing problems to tackle.

Problem 1: Smart Window/Blind System.
• Problem Statement: Energy consumption for heating, cooling, and lighting in buildings contributes significantly to operational costs and carbon emissions. Windows, in particular, play a large role in energy loss, with traditional designs providing little control over light and heat entry.
• Need: There is a need for a smart window system that can dynamically adjust its transparency based on external weather conditions and indoor temperature, reducing energy consumption for heating, cooling, and lighting while maintaining occupant comfort - i.e elderly, special needs etc

Problem 2: Bike Safety
• Problem Statement: Bicycle riders, especially in urban areas, are at high risk of accidents due to limited visibility, distractions, and poor communication with drivers and pedestrians.
• Need: There is a need for a product that enhances rider safety.

Problem 3: Autonomous Trash Collection Robot for Public Spaces
• Problem Statement: Public spaces such as parks and streets often accumulate litter, which can be costly and time-consuming to clean manually.
• Need: There is a need for an autonomous robot that can navigate public spaces, identify trash, and collect it, reducing the need for manual labor and keeping environments clean.
